Yeh tumhara Network Data Visualization (Task g) ka final graph hai. Aao iska "Post-Mortem" karte hain taaki tum ise easily explain kar sako.

Graph ka Basic Structure (Dancha)

  • Nodes (Dots): Har ek kaala dot (jaise Alice, Bob, Carol) ek insaan ko represent kar raha hai. Inki position X aur Y coordinates se aayi hai jo dataset mein "Row Type = Node" ke liye the.
  • Edges (Lines): Jo lines do dots ko jod rahi hain, unhe Edges kehte hain. Yeh lines bata rahi hain ki do logon ke beech mein connection hai.
  • Color (Relationship): Lines ka color bata raha hai ki un do logon ka rishta kaisa hai:
    • Blue: Colleague (Saath kaam karne wale)
    • Orange: Friend (Dost)
    • Red: Manager

Is graph se hamein kya kahani (Inference) pata chal rahi hai?

Network graph kisi bhi social group ya company ka "Androoni Jaal" (Internal Structure) batata hai:

  1. Sabse Zaroori Insaan (Central Node):
    Graph mein dekho, Alice aur Jack ke paas sabse zyada lines aa/ja rahi hain. Network ki bhasha mein inka "Degree Centrality" high hai. Iska matlab yeh is social group ke sabse popular ya connected log hain. Agar koi nayi khabar group mein phailani ho, toh Alice ya Jack ko bata do, sabko pata chal jayega!
  1. Manager ka Role (Hierarchy):
    Dhyan se dekho, is poore jaal mein sirf ek hi Red Line (Manager) hai, jo David aur Alice ke beech hai. (Jaisa ki code analysis mein bhi dikha Manager: 1.0). Yeh batata hai ki is poore group mein shayad David, Alice ka boss/manager hai, jabki baaki log aapas mein dost ya colleagues hain.
  1. Dosti aur Kaam ka Balance:
    Graph mein Orange (Friends) aur Blue (Colleagues) lines lagbhag barabar dikh rahi hain (13 Friends, 16 Colleagues). Iska matlab yeh ek aisi company ya group hai jahan log sirf saath kaam hi nahi karte, balki aapas mein achhe dost bhi hain.
  1. Cluster (Chhote Jhund):
    Agar tum Frank, Henry, aur Grace wale side ko dekho, toh wahan aapas mein kaafi connections hain. Yeh dikhata hai ki yeh teeno ka ek apna chhota sa 'gang' ya team hai is bade network ke andar.

Viva Tip: Ma'am poochein ki is graph ko banane mein Tableau mein kya trick lagi? Toh proudly batana: "Ma'am, Tableau natively Network graphs support nahi karta. Isliye maine data ko 'Nodes' aur 'Edges' mein divide kiya, X aur Y coordinates use karke dots banaye, aur fir 'Path Order' ka use karke ek line graph (Dual Axis) se un dots ko connect kiya. Yeh Dual Axis Data Visualization ki ek advanced technique hai."
